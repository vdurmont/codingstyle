What is this website?

It's a first, simple and basic attempt to create a form that could help you choose your coding style.

The goal is to ask you (and your team) the good questions and provide you with default answers if you don't really care about some setting.

The current version has something like 3 questions, it's just a POC to check how you would feel about a system like that.
